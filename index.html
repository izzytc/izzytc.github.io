Proposal


Te Papa Exhibition Rating App

I am creating a mobile app for Te Papa to personalize visitors experience. My mobile app will be an interactive feedback page where users are able to rate their own personal experiences they had in Te papa. I will have a star rating system with each exhibition. Te Papa consists of 6 levels, so this will
be reflected in how I layout my mobile app. You can use this app while you are in Te papa, as when you rate the different exhibitions the app will suggest other exhibition’s you would like depending on the rating. If you rate an exhibition very low then the app will ask you questions of why you didn’t like it or what can be improved etc,
which users are able to skip and can just go on to see the suggestions the app has suggested. There will also be a page for high-ranking exhibitions and this will allow users to see which exhibitions are getting rated really highly and which are getting rated poorly, users can also make a decision based on these statistics.




Interactions


I will be implementing a simple star rating system where users are able to touch how many stars they are wanting and swipe left and right if they want to go to the particular suggested exhibition. Once a user has clicked on an exhibition they can click that they are ‘done’ and are able to rate it. My app will work on mobile, ipad, and computer however the preferred method would be mobile and ipad due to the accessibility with these devices via walking around the Te Papa. However having it fit to all three devices allows Te Papa to take advantage of this and have this webpage accessible with computers around the museum, where people can use the app a bit differently. Visitors can rate some exhibitions that they have already been too and then the app can suggest more places to look around.



How it benefits Te Papa


This app also helps Te Papa as it is providing constant feedback from different users. Te papa will be constantly aware of what the public likes and dislikes, and can make informed decisions when making changes to the museum. Allowing users to post as anonymous will be very useful in improving the museum as users can speak freely.

Donations



The app will also have an easy user friendly donating system based on the rating you gave on a exhibition, if a user was to rate very high a donation pop up will come up which they can either skip or donate. This will hopefully increase visitor access, as visitors are able to go around the museum actually looking at what they like rather than spending time on ones they don't. This is an easy user-friendly way of getting good feedback

This also benefits Te Papa as more social media coverage is a good way of increasing visitors.
